Fruit3DGS

A General Fruit Counting and Localization Framework Leveraging Semantic-Guided 3D Gaussian Splatting and Contrastive Learning

Abstract

Fruit counting and localization are essential for yield estimation and robotic harvesting in modern orchards. Fruit3DGS introduces a semantic-guided 3D Gaussian Splatting framework combined with contrastive learning to reconstruct high-fidelity 3D models and extract individual fruit instances from unordered image collections. The framework produces semantically aware Gaussian primitives enriched with instance-discriminative embeddings, allowing robust clustering and accurate 3D localization for robotic harvesting tasks.
